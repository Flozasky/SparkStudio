Studio - Les humains derrière le design

Hey! I’m Yohji, a designer with a focus on creating thoughtful digital products. My design path started with a passion for visual art, which evolved into a deep interest in product design. These days, I work across various industries, helping businesses create meaningful experiences for their users. My experience ranges from building responsive websites to creating fully immersive user interfaces for web and mobile apps.When I'm not in design mode, I'm delving into fashion, history, or interior design. And yes, I've been building side projects too – quite a few have ended up in my own little project graveyard 🥀
